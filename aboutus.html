About Us

Our History

Started in 2010, Anis Hyderanad House quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in St Louis Missouri. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else. Featuring the best Dishes, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by Mr. Anil Reddy Jukanti.
